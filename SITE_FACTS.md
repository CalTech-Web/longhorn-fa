## FAQ Content

**Q: How long does it take to complete a fire alarm design?**
Projects up to 350 devices typically come back in five business days. If your schedule is tighter than that, we have expedited options.

**Q: Are you licensed to stamp fire alarm plans?**
Every set of drawings is stamped by a Texas-licensed professional and reviewed by a NICET IV certified engineer before it leaves our office.

**Q: Do you serve areas outside of Texas?**
We provide design services nationwide. The stamp is Texas, but we work to each state's local codes for projects across the country.

**Q: How much does a typical fire alarm design cost?**
Small projects start at $500. Standard plans start at $750, with add-ons at $7 per device and $50 for cut sheets. Every quote is flat-rate before work begins.

**Q: Do you charge hourly rates?**
No hourly billing, ever. You get a flat quote before we start, and that is the number on your invoice.

**Q: What file formats do you deliver?**
Standard delivery is PDF. CAD files are available on request. Printed copies can ship via UPS if you need them.
